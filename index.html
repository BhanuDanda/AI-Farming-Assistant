About KrishiAI

What is
KrishiAI?

KrishiAI is an AI-powered farming assistant designed to help Indian farmers make smarter decisions. Simply enter your soil type, region, and season — and get instant, science-backed advice on what to grow, what's wrong with your crops, and how to nourish your soil.

No agricultural degree needed. No expensive consultants. Just practical, personalised guidance — available 24/7, completely free.

🌱 Free for Farmers 📱 Works on Mobile 🇮🇳 India-First Design
